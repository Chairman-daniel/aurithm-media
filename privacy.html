Privacy Policy

At Aurithm Media, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, subscribe, or contact us, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing details.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may automatically collect data like IP address, browser type, operating system, and website usage patterns.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To send marketing and promotional communications (with your consent).
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve website functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers and partners who assist in delivering our services.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • In the event of a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aurithmmedia.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
